description: Score your vault's health and get improvement tips
---

Run `zed health` via the Bash tool to get a health score for the knowledge vault.

Present the results clearly — the score, grade, metrics, and any recommendations for improving the knowledge graph.

If the score is low, proactively suggest specific actions the user can take (e.g., "You have 5 orphan notes — want me to suggest connections for them?").
